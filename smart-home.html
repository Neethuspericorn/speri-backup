How does the Smart Home app work?

The Smart Home app connects to the users surveillance camera or security system at home or preferred site. The app is then paired with Bluetooth-compatible devices like speakers, lights, smartwatches or earphones.

The app alerts the connected Bluetooth device when the camera detects motion or objects. These alerts can be in various forms, depending on the users preferences and needs. The app provides audio notifications for visually impaired individuals, conveying information about the detected motion or objects. The app utilizes alternative methods for hard-of-hearing individuals, such as light signals, text notifications, and vibration alerts, to ensure they receive the necessary signals.
