Life @ Spericorn Technology

Our largest campus is in Technopark, we do it all, and so can you. Team SPERICORN embraces ideas and has an entrepreneurial spark, and it's a dream company for many. We aim to create an inclusive, transparent, and socially responsible workplace culture that allows everyone to reach their full potential. As a team of efficient and modernized individuals, we strive to deliver the best products and services to our clients. Together we create millions of great memories by celebrating Birthdays & Gift Voucher presentations, team gatherings, and Outings. Small moments between work and crazy hard-on parties are never missed. We create a better space for our team members to work pleasantly.
